BUY WOMEN'S CASUAL SLIPPERS ONLINE AT AMAZON INDIA

Casual slippers go well with most attires, be it a gown, sari, salwar, or denims. These are ideal daily wear, which can keep your feet cool and dry. Though they’re termed as casual slippers, there are a lot of variations in designs. They can be so plain that it can be worn only for household activities, to slippers and sandals that are rich in design and can be worn for special occasions and parties.

The basic definition of a slipper is that it is light footwear, which is easy and comfortable to put on and take off, and suitable to be worn indoors. But over the years, slippers have evolved so much beyond this. There are different types of slippers like slip-ons, slipper boots, sandal slippers, flip flops, and closed slippers. These can be worn both indoors and outdoors and are suitable for plenty of occasions.

You can now buy shoes online in India in the comfort of your home. Various kinds of casual shoes can be browsed, compared, and bought at Amazon India. Many leading shoe and slipper brands like HM, Carlton London, Gliders, Rawhide, Catwalk, Adidas, Inc. 5, and Mochi can be found while online shoe shopping.

Casual slippers online do not usually have an ankle strap. While some straps have a toe partition, others just cover the instep. The heels of these slippers also vary, from high-pointed heels to high wedge heels and flat heels. The major difference you will find amongst these slippers is the design pattern on the straps. While some are just plain straps, others are multicoloured and designed like flowers or embellished with stones. Some slippers also come in suede, which is great for fashionable occasions. If you want to buy casual slippers online, Amazon India provides a wholesome selection.
