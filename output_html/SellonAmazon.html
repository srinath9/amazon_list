Features and Benefits

Selling on Amazon helps you expand your business nationwide and gain access to a state-of-the-art logistics network. You can list an unlimited number of products and benefit from Amazon’s investment in technology, logistics and a secure payment infrastructure. Additionally, you will have access to Amazon’s proven seller coaching guides and services that help you list your products on Seller Central, manage your account, access reports and respond to buyers.

Sell on Amazon comes enabled with Amazon Easy Ship , a pickup and delivery Service for Amazon.in sellers. Buyers get track-able shipments with reliable and consistent delivery time. Buyers also get an option to pay for orders at the time of delivery using the Cash on Delivery (COD) feature.

With Sell on Amazon, you also opt into our pay-per-use fulfillment service called Fulfilment by Amazon (FBA). With FBA, you store your products in our warehouses and we pack, ship and deliver your products to the buyers while managing customer service. With the trusted Amazon platform and fulfillment services, you will have access to the resources you need to build your business

High quality images and content provide better customer experience that in turn, is known to directly impact chances of incremental sales. Amazon's service Provider Network can help you prepare high-quality, high-impact listings for your Amazon business.

Benefits at a glance:

  • Open a new sales channel and reach nationwide potential customers
  • Convenient shopping experience for customers
  • Benefit from Amazon's investment in technology and innovation
  • No product listing fees
  • No upfront costs of creating a website or setting up a physical store
  • Secure payments
