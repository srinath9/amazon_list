4.0 out of 5 stars Useful even for reading Journal papers 4 January 2015
By Kundan Krishna TOP 500 REVIEWER
Verified Purchase
I bought this as I had to read a lot of research papers and ebooks on my laptop (I'm an undergraduate student).
The good parts:
1> Awesome e-ink display.Say goodbye to screen glare.
2>Quite fast page loading, atleast for .mobi formats. And even for pdfs containing images (I used it to read Nagraj's comics: about 7 MB in size).
3>Lightweight: No need to carry an 800 page book in your bag.
4>It has an experimental browser to surf the net.
5>Quite long battery backup
6>Touch is awesome (IR touchscreen, can be operated with your gloves on. You can use anything as a stylus since it isn't a capacitive touch)

The negatives:
1> Very slight reflection of external light can be seen on the screen. But only when you tilt it against light at a slant angle. Nothing to worry about. By adjusting the angle a bit, it quickly vanishes.
2>PDFs are rendered well, but it is very uncomfortable to browse a pdf while having it zoomed. You can try the landscape mode to make the fonts bigger.
3>The experimental browser is OK for facebook and gmail. But it slows down on heavy sites and even crashes/hangs at times.
4> Cannot download PDF using the inbuilt experimental browser.
5>Can't open browser in landscape mode.

Verdict:
A very nice device at this price range. If you want to read novels, you'll have no complaints. Any PDF file with large enough fonts will be very comfortable to read. If you want to read IEEE-style two column research papers, or read a pdf book containing a lot of equations (in pdf format), you can use a free tool called k2pdfopt to convert into a Kindle optimized single-column version. I've tried it and it works very well most of the time.

By Aditya Vardhan Sharma
Verified Purchase
I think that at 6000 bucks, this Kindle offers the best deal out of all. I've been using it for about 4 days now.

I also compared it to a friend's Paperwhite and found the following differences:

Overall, this is a good product and for 5000 lesser, it is a better deal than the PaperWhite. More value for money.
