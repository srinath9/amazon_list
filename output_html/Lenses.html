Types of Camera Lenses

Wide Angle Lenses

Wide angle lenses are perfect for capturing landscapes, architecture and large groups of people. There are three types of wide angle lenses - wide, ultra-wide and fish-eye. A wide lens is classified as anything shorter than 35mm.

Normal Lenses

Ideal for travel and street photography, normal lenses offer a focal length of 35mm to 50mm. These lenses are the most commonly used type of lenses.

Telephoto Lenses

Telephoto lenses are mostly used for wildlife and sports photography. These lenses bring distant objects closer and offer a focal length of anything in between 135mm to 300mm.

Prime Lenses

Offering a fixed focal length and a larger aperture, prime lenses are used for indoor and low-light photography.

Zoom Lenses

Designed to give a wide focal range, zoom lenses are mostly used by travel enthusiasts and hobbyists. These lenses allow you to easily switch from normal to telephoto or ultra-wide to wide for a versatile photography experience.

Macro Lenses

Ideal for still-life photography of small objects, macro lenses offer an intense level of magnification.
