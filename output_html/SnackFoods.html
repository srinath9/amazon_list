Munch on your favourite snack while watching an exciting football match or reading a thrilling book. Be it delicious chocolate chip biscuits, crunchy potato wafers or healthy sugar free biscuits that you wish to buy; you will find all this and more at the online store.

Crunchy Chips, Wafers and Puffed Snacks

Amazon India features a huge selection of puffed snacks, chips and wafers that is sure to delight you. Whether you are taking a break at work or enjoying your weekend at home, these tasty chips and puffed snacks will help you sustain till you eat a larger meal. These crispy munchies make the ideal teatime snack and the perfect bite-sized delicacy for your house party. From mouth watering onion rings, cheese balls to classic potato chips and salted tortilla chips, the online store has everything that you would need to stock up your snack supplies.

Delicious Crackers, Biscuits and Cookies

Carry your favourite chocolate chip biscuit or salting cracker while you travel around or head out for a picnic with your family and friends. At Amazon India, you will find butter cookies, oat biscuits, coconut biscuits, hazelnut cookies, caramel biscuits, sugarless cookies, wheat crackers, vegetable crackers and a variety of other types of cookies and biscuits online. Made using premium ingredients, these exquisite crackers, biscuits and cookies are sure to melt in your mouth and offer you a delightful snack time. You can either have these cookies alone or along with a cup of tea or coffee to give yourself an indulgent treat.

Healthy Granola Bars and Trail Mix Snacks

Keep your body energised at all times by chewing into a healthy and scrumptious granola bar. An easy and portable breakfast or snack option, these bars can be easily carried around so that you can eat them whenever you feel hungry. Available in different flavours and textures, these bars will make a great addition to your travel bag. Alternatively, you can also have a handful of trail mix snacks to give yourself a quick energy boost. Featuring a mix of cashews, raisins, tropical nuts and fruits, trail mix snacks make a healthy snacking option for both children as well as adults.

Delightful Cakes, Cup Cakes, Mousse and More

Pamper your sweet tooth with the wide selection of delicious cup-cakes, chocolate mousse and Swiss rolls available at Amazon India. Offering a variety of mouth-watering flavours such as strawberry, vanilla, black currant and chocolate, these cakes are sure to enhance your snack time experience. You will also find an assortment of pretzels, pretzel sticks, popcorn, breadsticks, assorted biscuits, crisp bread, sugar free biscuits and rice cakes at the online store.
