Shop online for Large Appliances

With electronic appliances becoming quintessential in every household, you cannot simply ignore any of them as they help in making every day in your life just easier. They are not only functional, but also sophisticated and stylish as well. Amazon India offers a wide range of large appliances such as refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners, dishwashers, microwaves and water purifiers from leading brands including Godrej, Croma, Blue Star, Carrier, Electrolux, Bosch, Gorenje, Samsung, Whirlpool, LG, Kenmore and GE.

Refrigerators - Your Life Saver!

Can you imagine your kitchen without a refrigerator? From preserving fruits, vegetables and meat to storing leftovers, it tops the list of the must-have items in a kitchen. There are different types of refrigerators available at this online store. Whether you are planning to replace the old one or setting up your new home, you can choose the one that best suits your kitchen. From different capacities and types to Energy Star certified models, find everything under one roof at Amazon India.

Single Door Refrigerators - Single door refrigerators are ideal for compact homes, small offices, grocery shops and other commercial stores. They are designed with a single door and perfectly seal the cold air inside. Single door refrigerators are space saving and economical as well. They comprise of a main compartment, freezer compartment, vegetable bin and small shelves inside the door.

Double Door Refrigerators - Perfect for a big family, double door refrigerators offer more space to keep your food items. They have a separate fridge compartment and a freezer compartment at the top. Some models feature the freezer compartment at the bottom. Double door refrigerators ensure only less air escapes, so the energy consumption will also be reduced.

Side-by-Side Door Refrigerators - Side-by-side door refrigerators are usually large. Although they are functional, side-by-side door refrigerators add a sense of style to your kitchen. They offer ample room for stor1ing food items, beverage bottles, vegetables, fruits, milk and more. Side-by-side door refrigerators feature an LCD display to show temperature and adjust settings. Some of the models are designed with a water and ice dispenser, ensuring versatile features.

Washing Machines - Doing laundry every day can be a tiresome task. Washing machines come to your rescue to make washing clothes a lot simpler. You can find both top loading and front loading washing machines at Amazon India.

Top Loading Washing Machines

Top loading washing machines are compactly designed to save space. But they boast a large capacity to hold more clothes. Top loading washing machines are integrated with user-friendly features and are simple to use. They cost less than front loading washing machines.

Front Loading Washing Machines

Front loading washing machines are so popular these days for their large washing capacity and improved wash functions. They offer a number of programs to deliver superior washing performance. In addition, front loading washing machines are water and energy efficient, making them an environmentally friendly option.

Air Conditioners

One of the ideal options to beat the heat, air conditioners help make your room cool and comfortable. Select from two types of air conditioners and capacities for your home at Amazon India. You can choose air conditioners according to your room’s size.

Split ACs

Split air conditioners offer powerful cooling performance. They compromise of two units called internal and external units. Split air conditioners can be conveniently installed in compact homes. The internal unit is easy to set up on the wall and it does not take much space. Split air conditioners are energy efficient to cut down on your electric bills.

Window ACs

As the name implies, window air conditioners are easy to fit in a window. You can opt for window ACs when you don’t have the provision to place split air conditioners at your home. Window air conditioners are economical than split air conditioners and are very simple to install.

Dishwashers

Cleaning dishes is another tiring task in every household. You can save time on washing your dishes by choosing the right dishwasher for your home. You can find dishwashers with different capacities and types from popular brands at Amazon India. Choose dishwashers according to your family’s size and most of them come equipped with improved functionalities, programs and technology to make life easier.

Browse through the huge collection of large appliances at Amazon India and get the right ones for your home.
