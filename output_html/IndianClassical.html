Indian Classical Music On Amazon.in

Every Indian loves a classical tune every now and then. Whether it’s Carnatic music or Hindustani music, classical music is derived from the ragas and is a great way to go back to your roots.

Like with any other music category you’ll find on this site, Indian classical music is a genre that has been explored to the fullest. Find popular singles and albums from some of the most prominent artists in the genre. This includes classic artists like Pt. Jasraj, Pt. Hari Prasad Chaurasia, Ustad Zakir Hussain, Pt. Bhimsen Joshi, Ustad Bismillah Khan, D. K. Pattamal, M. S. Subbulakshmi, N. C. Vasanthakokilam, Muthiah Bhagvathar, Mysore Vasudevachar, Chembai Vaidyanatha Bhagavathar, and more. And then, there are new age artists as well, such as Nithyashree Mahadevan, P. Unnikrishnan, Vishaka Hari, R. Suryaprakash and K. V. Krishna Prasad.

No matter what your specific tastes in Indian classical music are, you’re sure to find hours of music that you will enjoy, right here. If you’re a fan of carnatic music, you can find the best of raga alpanas, kalpanaswarams, niravals, tanams and more. And if you’re a fan of Hindustani classical music, you can find the best khyals and dhrupads right here.

Indian classical music can be found in many different languages as well. There’s no need to restrict yourself only to the most commonly heard of languages, when shopping for music online. Pick out your favorite song, no matter what language it’s been sung in. There’s Hindi, Malayalam, Kannada, Tamil, and more. And you’ll find music from the very best artists that sing in these languages too.

Once you’ve picked out your favorite artist, genre and even language, there’s still some room to help you shop for exactly what you need. All music is available in many different formats. This includes Audio CDs, MP3s and Vinyl. Find exactly the kind of music you need at Amazon.in and purchase it at reasonable prices.
