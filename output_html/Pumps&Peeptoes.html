BUY PUMPS & PEEPTOES ONLINE AT AMAZON INDIA

If you’re a lady with an eye for haute couture fashion, you can’t go without owning a pair of pumps and peeptoes. Whether you’re donned in a chic dress for a party or meeting clients, these ladies shoes are sure to turn heads. While most of them complement your outfit when you dress up for special occasions, there are some designs that will make you looking stylish without trying too hard. Pumps and peeptoes can serve as daily wear too.

Pumps, also known as court shoes, are shoes that are low-cut on the front usually don't have a fastening. Some have an ankle strap, though. They are quintessential shoes for women, preferred usually by those who are fashion-conscious. Pumps are made of various materials, but the traditional ones are made with patent leather. They go well with different kinds of outfits like dresses, trousers, skirts, and jeans. Stilettos, which are a norm in beauty pageants and classy parties, are a variation of pumps. They have high heels, and depending on the occasion, they can be worn as formal shoes or informal ones. Peeptoes are a variety of pumps, with an opening at the toe-box, allowing the toes to be seen.

You can now buy pumps and peeptoes, with the comfort of your home by shopping online. Here at Amazon India, you can find world-class brands like Carlton London, Catwalk, Cobblerz, Rawhide, Jove, Clarks, Tresmode, and Senorita offering you a variety of pumps and peep toes. They differ in style, colour, and design. While some are simple with plain single-coloured design and small heels, others are grand with shiny finish, stiletto heels, and suede leather.
