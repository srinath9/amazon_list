BUY MEN'S SANDALS AND FLOATERS ONLINE AT AMAZON INDIA

Whether you’re hopping on to your friend’s bike for a quick ride to the nearby coffee shop, attending university, walking in a park, or spending the day shopping, sandals and floaters will be your best bet for comfortable and happy feet. At Amazon India, you will find a colossal range of sandals and floaters suitable for different age groups and tastes. Choose from various brands, best prices, and exclusive deals.

Sandals and floaters are an open type of footwear, suitable for outdoor purposes. The basic design of sandals or floaters is that of a sole attached to the foot of the wearer, with straps passing around the ankle and over the instep. The main feature that separates sandals and floaters from other types of footwear is that a sandal or floater exposes the skin of most of the upper part of the foot, especially the toes.

Sandals and floaters are usually worn by people in warm and hot regions, as it helps to keep the feet cool and dry with sufficient air circulation. Certain kinds of foot infections can also be lowered by wearing sandals or floaters.

You can find synthetic sandals and floaters online from various leading brands like Puma, Fila, Woodland, Sparx, Adidas, Reebok, Bata, Pavers England and many more. These are made of materials like polyurethane and nylon. These have a vibrant and youthful look, and can be worn casually. Apart from synthetic materials, you can find sandals and floaters made of rubber. Some are also made of faux leather. Various colours like red, blue, green, black, and yellow are seen in these synthetic sandals.

While synthetic sandals and floaters give a casual and youthful look, leather ones exude a matured look. Brands that produce leather sandals and floaters include Woodland, Lee Cooper, Ruosh, Red Tape, Buckaroo, and Clarks. Some of these sandals and floaters have ankle straps, and are available in colours like brown and black. They can be worn with Western outfits like denims and trousers or Indian clothing like dhoti. From young adults to old men, leather sandals, and floaters will be suitable for a wide age group.
