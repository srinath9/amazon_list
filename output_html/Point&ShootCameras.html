Buy Point & Shoot Digital Cameras Online at Amazon India

Point & Shoot Digital Cameras or compact cameras are popular for their easy operation and simple controls. These digital cameras have been a conventional choice for simple still photography. Being small and compact, point & shoot cameras will easily fit into your pocket, backpack or purse.

What to Look for in a Point & Shoot Digital Camera

Camera Resolution

You will find a range of resolution options at hand. Basic point & shoot cameras come with a decent resolution of up to 11.9 MP, the high-end ones have resolutions of 20 MP and above, and the others are placed in between.

Optical Zoom

One of the key features of point & shoot cameras is their optical zoom lens. A 20x to 30x and above optical zoom capability (Megazoom) with wide-angle lens and advanced auto-focus system is perfect for capturing close-up wildlife shots, sports and landscapes. If you need a camera for everyday photography tasks, the 3x to 5.9x models (basic point & shoot) or 10x to 20x models (Pocket Megazoom) with around 28mm wide-angle coverage can give you good results.

Display Size

Another feature to watch out for while buying a camera is the display size. Compact cameras have a smaller display size but offer better portability for casual users; whereas the larger display size models are great options to capture scenery shots.

Image Stabilization

Handheld shooting often results in camera shakes or blurry shots. Many of these point & shoot digital cameras incorporate the IS (Image Stabilization) feature to eliminate blurs and to give you detailed, clear images even in low-light conditions.

Viewfinder Type

Point & shoot cameras come with or without an electronic viewfinder. The viewfinder provides live previews of shots, so that you can aim the camera in a better manner.
